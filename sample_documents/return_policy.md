# TechStore Return and Exchange Policy

## Return Period

### Standard Products
- **30-day return window** from the date of delivery
- Product must be in original condition with all accessories and packaging
- Proof of purchase required (order number or receipt)

### Opened Electronics
- Laptops and computers: Can be returned within 15 days if unopened, 7 days if opened
- Smartphones and tablets: Can be returned within 14 days if unopened, 7 days if opened
- Accessories: 30 days return window

### Non-Returnable Items
- Software and digital downloads (once activated)
- Gift cards
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Personalized or customized products

## Return Process

### Step 1: Initiate Return
1. Log into your TechStore account
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Select the item you want to return
4. Click "Request Return" and select a reason
5. You'll receive a return authorization number (RMA) within 24 hours

### Step 2: Package the Item
- Include all original accessories, manuals, and packaging
- Print and include the return label we'll email you
- Pack securely to prevent damage during shipping

### Step 3: Ship the Item
- Drop off at any authorized shipping location
- Keep the tracking number for your records
- We recommend insuring valuable items

### Step 4: Refund Processing
- We inspect the item within 3-5 business days of receiving it
- Refund is processed within 5-7 business days after inspection
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable (except for defective items)

## Exchange Policy

### Product Exchange
- Available for defective or damaged items
- Contact customer support within 7 days of receiving the item
- We'll send a replacement immediately (subject to availability)
- No additional charges for exchanges due to defects

### Different Model Exchange
- If you want a different model, you must return the original and place a new order
- Price difference will be charged or refunded accordingly

## Restocking Fees

### Standard Returns
- No restocking fee for unopened items
- 15% restocking fee for opened electronics in perfect condition
- No fee for defective or damaged items

### Special Cases
- Custom-ordered items: 25% restocking fee
- Bulk orders (5+ items): 20% restocking fee

## Defective or Damaged Items

### Immediate Reporting
- Report defects or damage within 48 hours of delivery
- Take photos of the damaged item and packaging
- Contact customer support with your order number and photos

### Resolution Options
1. **Full refund** - Return the item for a complete refund including shipping
2. **Replacement** - We'll send a new item at no additional cost
3. **Repair** - For minor issues, we may offer a repair service

### Our Responsibility
- We cover all return shipping costs for defective items
- No restocking fees apply
- Expedited replacement shipping available

## International Returns

### Process
- Contact customer support before returning
- Customer pays return shipping costs
- Customs duties and taxes are non-refundable
- Refunds processed in original currency

### Timeline
- May take 10-15 business days for international returns
- Currency exchange rates may affect refund amount

## Warranty vs Return

### Return Period (30 days)
- Full refund or exchange
- No questions asked (within policy guidelines)

### Warranty Period (After 30 days)
- Covered by manufacturer warranty
- Repairs or replacements through warranty service
- See warranty documentation for details

## Special Circumstances

### Holiday Returns
- Items purchased between November 1 and December 25 can be returned until January 15
- Standard return policy terms apply

### Extended Return Option
- Available for Premium Members (60-day return window)
- $49 annual membership fee
- Additional benefits included

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I return an item without the original packaging?**
A: We prefer items to be returned in original packaging, but we accept returns without it. However, a restocking fee may apply.

**Q: How long does the refund take?**
A: 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the item, plus 3-5 days for your bank to process.

**Q: Can I exchange for a different color or size?**
A: Yes! Follow the normal return process and place a new order for the desired variant.

**Q: What if I received the wrong item?**
A: Contact us immediately. We'll send the correct item with a prepaid return label for the wrong item.

**Q: Do I have to pay for return shipping?**
A: Yes, unless the item is defective or we shipped the wrong item.
