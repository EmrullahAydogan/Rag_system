# TechStore Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner, enter your email and create a password. You'll receive a confirmation email - click the link to activate your account.

### Do I need an account to make a purchase?
No, you can checkout as a guest. However, having an account allows you to track orders, save addresses, and access order history.

### How can I contact customer support?
- **Phone:** 1-800-TECH-STORE (1-800-832-4786)
- **Email:** support@techstore.com
- **Live Chat:** Available 24/7 on our website
- **Response Time:** Within 24 hours

## Ordering

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Klarna (Buy Now, Pay Later)
- TechStore Gift Cards

### Can I cancel my order?
Yes, if the order hasn't shipped yet. Contact us within 1 hour of placing the order for guaranteed cancellation. After shipment, you'll need to initiate a return.

### Can I modify my order after placing it?
Contact us immediately. If the order hasn't been processed, we can modify the shipping address or items. After processing, modifications aren't possible.

### What is your price matching policy?
We match prices from authorized retailers for identical items. Contact us within 7 days of purchase with proof of lower price. Price match doesn't apply to marketplace sellers, auction sites, or closeout sales.

## Products

### Are all products brand new?
Yes, all products are brand new unless specifically marked as "Refurbished" or "Open Box." Refurbished items are tested and certified with a 90-day warranty.

### Do you sell internationally branded products?
All products are designed for the US market with US warranties. Some items may work internationally, but we can't guarantee compatibility.

### How do I know if a product is in stock?
Product pages show real-time stock status:
- "In Stock" - Ships within 1 business day
- "Low Stock" - Limited quantity available
- "Out of Stock" - Not currently available
- "Pre-Order" - Not yet released

### Can I pre-order upcoming products?
Yes! We offer pre-orders for major product launches. You'll only be charged when the item ships. Pre-order prices are guaranteed (no price increases).

## Shipping and Delivery

### How long does shipping take?
- Standard (Free over $50): 5-7 business days
- Express ($14.99): 2-3 business days
- Overnight ($29.99): Next business day
- Same-Day (Select cities, $19.99): Within 4 hours

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 100 countries. International shipping rates and times vary by destination. Customer is responsible for customs duties and taxes.

### What if my package is lost or stolen?
Contact us immediately. We'll open an investigation with the carrier. If confirmed lost, we'll send a replacement or issue a refund. All shipments are insured.

### Can I track my order?
Yes! You'll receive a tracking number via email within 24 hours of shipment. Track on our website or directly on the carrier's website.

## Returns and Refunds

### What is your return policy?
30-day return window for most items. Products must be in original condition with all accessories. Opened electronics have a 7-15 day return window depending on the category.

### How do I return an item?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Order History
3. Select the item and click "Request Return"
4. Print the return label we email you
5. Ship the item back

### When will I receive my refund?
Refunds are processed 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. It may take an additional 3-5 days for your bank to process the credit.

### Are there any restocking fees?
- No fee for unopened items
- 15% fee for opened electronics in perfect condition
- No fee for defective items
