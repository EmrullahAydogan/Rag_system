# TechStore Warranty Terms and Conditions

## Standard Warranty Coverage

### Laptops and Computers
- **Duration:** 2 years from purchase date
- **Coverage:**
  - Manufacturing defects
  - Hardware component failures
  - Battery defects (replacement if capacity drops below 80% in first year)
- **Exclusions:**
  - Accidental damage
  - Water damage
  - Software issues
  - Cosmetic damage not affecting functionality

### Smartphones and Tablets
- **Duration:** 1 year from purchase date
- **Coverage:**
  - Manufacturing defects
  - Screen defects (dead pixels, discoloration)
  - Battery defects (replacement if capacity drops below 70% in first year)
  - Charging port issues
- **Exclusions:**
  - Cracked screens from drops
  - Water damage (even on water-resistant models)
  - Lost or stolen devices
  - Jailbroken or rooted devices

### Accessories (Headphones, Chargers, Cables)
- **Duration:** 1 year from purchase date
- **Coverage:**
  - Manufacturing defects
  - Component failures under normal use
- **Exclusions:**
  - Physical damage
  - Wear and tear
  - Lost items

### Smart Watches
- **Duration:** 1 year from purchase date
- **Coverage:**
  - Manufacturing defects
  - Display issues
  - Sensor malfunctions
  - Battery defects
- **Exclusions:**
  - Cracked screens
  - Band wear and tear
  - Water damage beyond rating

## Warranty Service Process

### Step 1: Verify Warranty Status
- Check warranty status on our website using serial number
- Ensure issue is covered under warranty terms
- Gather proof of purchase

### Step 2: Contact Support
- **Phone:** 1-800-WARRANTY (1-800-927-7268)
- **Email:** warranty@techstore.com
- **Live Chat:** Available on our website
- Describe the issue in detail
- Have your order number and serial number ready

### Step 3: Diagnostics
- Our technician will guide you through basic troubleshooting
- May require diagnostic software installation
- Remote support session may be scheduled

### Step 4: Service Options

**Option A: Mail-In Service**
- We'll email you a prepaid shipping label
- Pack device securely with RMA number
- Drop off at authorized shipping location
- Repair time: 7-10 business days after receipt

**Option B: Walk-In Service** (Select locations)
- Schedule appointment at nearest service center
- Bring device and proof of purchase
- Same-day service for minor repairs
- 3-5 days for major repairs

**Option C: On-Site Service** (Premium members only)
- Available for laptops and computers
- Technician visits your location
- Scheduled within 48 hours
- Most repairs completed on-site

### Step 5: Repair or Replacement

**Repair**
- Device will be repaired with genuine parts
- 90-day warranty on repaired components
- All data will be preserved when possible

**Replacement**
- If repair is not feasible, device will be replaced
- Replacement may be refurbished (same or better condition)
- If model unavailable, comparable model provided
- Data transfer assistance available

## What Voids the Warranty

### Unauthorized Modifications
- Opening the device (tamper-proof seal broken)
- Third-party repairs
- Hardware modifications
- Overclocking beyond manufacturer specifications

### Misuse and Abuse
- Physical damage from drops or impacts
- Liquid damage
- Exposure to extreme temperatures
- Using non-approved accessories
- Operating outside environmental specifications

### Software Violations
- Installing unauthorized firmware
- Jailbreaking or rooting
- Removing pre-installed operating system
- Virus or malware damage

## Battery Warranty

### Coverage Terms
- Batteries are consumable items and degrade over time
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects only

### Replacement Criteria

**Laptops:**
- If capacity drops below 80% in first year: Free replacement
- If capacity drops below 80% in second year: Replacement at 50% cost

**Smartphones and Tablets:**
- If capacity drops below 70% in first year: Free replacement
- After first year: Replacement at cost

**Smart Watches:**
- If capacity drops below 70% in first year: Free replacement
- After first year: Replacement at cost

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to register my product for warranty?**
A: No, but we recommend it for faster service. Register on our website using your serial number.

**Q: What if I don't have my receipt?**
A: We can look up your purchase using your email or phone number. If ordered from another retailer, manufacturer warranty still applies.

**Q: Can I get a replacement immediately?**
A: Premium members can get expedited replacement. Standard warranty requires inspection first (3-5 days).

**Q: Does warranty cover software issues?**
A: No, but we offer free technical support for software problems within first 90 days.

**Q: What happens to my data during repair?**
A: We attempt to preserve data, but we recommend backing up before service. We're not responsible for data loss.

**Q: Can I upgrade my warranty later?**
A: Extended warranty must be purchased within 30 days of original purchase.
