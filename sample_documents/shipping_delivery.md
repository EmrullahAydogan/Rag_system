## Delivery Details

### Signature Requirements
- **Orders over $500:** Signature required
- **All smartphones and laptops:** Signature required
- **Other items:** Signature optional (can be requested)

### Delivery Locations
- **Residential:** Standard delivery to front door
- **Business:** Delivery during business hours (9AM-5PM)
- **PO Boxes:** Available for small items only
- **APO/FPO:** Available (processing may take additional 2-3 days)

### Delivery Instructions
- Add special instructions during checkout
- Examples: "Leave with neighbor," "Deliver to side door," "Call upon arrival"
- Drivers will follow instructions when possible and safe

### Failed Delivery Attempts
- Carrier makes 3 delivery attempts
- Notice left at delivery address
- Package held at local facility for pickup (5-7 days)
- After holding period, package returns to TechStore
- Reshipping fee may apply

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change my shipping address after ordering?**
A: Yes, if order hasn't shipped yet. Contact us ASAP. After shipment, contact carrier directly.

**Q: Do you ship on weekends?**
A: We don't ship on weekends, but carriers may deliver on Saturdays (depending on service level).

**Q: What if I'm not home for delivery?**
A: Carrier will leave notice. You can arrange redelivery or pickup at carrier facility.

**Q: Can I expedite an order already placed?**
A: Yes, if it hasn't shipped yet. Contact us within 1 hour of ordering. Shipping upgrade fees apply.

**Q: How is shipping calculated?**
A: Based on weight, dimensions, destination, and shipping speed selected.

**Q: Do shipping times include weekends?**
A: No, business days only (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays).
